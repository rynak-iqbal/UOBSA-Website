Studying in Ottawa


Study in Canada as an international student


https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/study-canada.html


Government of Canada resources for individuals planning to study in Canada. Explore details on the application process for study permits, options for extending them, opportunities for international student scholarships, as well as guidelines for student employment in Canada, and more.



Canadian Bangladesh Education Trust (CBET)


https://www.cbet.ca/


A registered CRA charity that provides support for Bangladeshi students and Ottawa-Gatineau high school and university students. They offer scholarship opportunities to aid Bangladeshi students in Ottawa-Gatineau who require financial assistance and seek guidance in integrating into Canadian society.



Financial aid


//www.uottawa.ca/study/fees-financial-support/student-accounts/payment-fees


A summary of how payments and fees work in relation at the University of Ottawa. You can take a look at important information about tuition fees, methods to make payments, important deadlines, and the various financial assistance options available.



Housing rentals


https://rentals.ca/


A platform to search for off-campus housing options. The filter feature can be used to narrow down to student housing options specifically.


Bangladeshi community


Bangladeshi Canadians-Canadian Bangladeshis (BCCB) - Ottawa Chapter


https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/study-canada.html


A Canadian community organization catering to individuals of Bangladeshi descent, serving as a hub for mutual support among its members. They organize diverse events catering to all age groups and offer an abundance of resources to assist the Bangladeshi community, including students, in Canada.
