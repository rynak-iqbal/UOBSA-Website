Welcome to the University of Ottawa's Bangladeshi Students' Association!

We are a student-run club aimed at connecting Bengali students all across Ottawa. Our events range from simple gatherings for chai and board games to spectacles with performances and an accompanying dance floor. Our focus in any event is to collectively indulge in our vast and vibrant culture and teach it to any who would like to learn. That means bring your friends and loved ones to any of our upcoming events for a night of fun! Learn more at our Events page.
